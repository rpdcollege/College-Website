About the college

Royal Prudence Degree College, Lakhimpur-Kheri is a self-finance Degree College established in July 2004 by Dr. JANARDAN SINGH, Founder Manager (Retd. H.O.D - Hindi Dept., Yuvraj Dutt Post Graduate College, Lakhimpur-Kheri U.P.) and his wife Dr. KAMINI SINGH (Retd. H.O.D. Hindi Deptt., Y.D.P.G. College, Lakhimpur-Kheri.)

The serene and beautiful campus of RPDC, Lakhimpur is spread over 5 acres of level land situated in the fields on Sitapur - Lakhimpur State Highway SH-21.

The College has established its credibility by giving more than 90 to 95 Percent result in all the streams since 2004. It has set the Academic standards to a new high. It provides Professional competence and enriches the Skills of the students to make them suitable for different Jobs available in the market. The students are encouraged to start their own enterprise and provide jobs to others.
